Providing you quality care and compassionate services.

Total Quality Residential Services, Inc. aims to provide comprehensive residential support services for youth and adult with an intellectual and developmental disability in the state of Virginia, Maryland, and in the District of Columbia. These services are dedicated to care and support for the whole person by providing them training, education, and access to community resources to help them achieve the best possible health and independence. We seek to help these people reach their full potential so they can become active members of their community without regard to their abilities. Here are the services we offer based on every location:

Maryland
We accept children from 0 to age 21 who have conditions ranging from mild prenatal drug exposure to the most complex conditions, such as ventilator dependence. Other common health issues served by our program include seizure disorders, cerebral palsy, shaken baby syndrome, reactive airway disease, and HIV/AIDS. We also accept adults with intellectual and/or physical disabilities.
